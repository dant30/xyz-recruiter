Document Requirements

Everything you need to prepare for your Gulf job application

Essential Documents

To process your Gulf job application, you'll need to provide the following documents:

Valid Passport

Must have at least 6 months validity from date of travel. Include clear copies of the bio-data page.

Birth Certificate

Original and copy from the Registrar of Persons. Must match passport details exactly.

Police Clearance Certificate

Certificate of Good Conduct from CID Headquarters. Valid for 6 months from issue date.

Medical Fitness Certificate

Must be from a Gulf-approved hospital. Includes chest X-ray, blood tests, and full physical examination.

Professional Certificates

For skilled positions (electricians, plumbers, etc.), include all relevant training certificates.

Additional Notes

Important Information

All documents must be clear, legible scans or photos. Blurry or incomplete documents will delay your application processing.

Document Processing Timeline

Passport Processing

7-14 working days for new applications

Police Clearance

10-21 working days for processing

Medical Tests

2-3 days for results at approved hospitals
